Certifications

Cardinal Innovator
Cardinal Innovatorclose

iDIETS (Intelligently Directed Innovation to Enable Technology for Sustainability) has introduced innovative aquaponics systems, addressing space constraints in conventional farming. Utilizing aero-based or vertical tower systems, the technology enhances efficiency, requiring minimal human intervention. The aim is to revolutionize modern farming for both aquaculture and agriculture.

Sci-Math Power Quiz
Sci-Math Power Quizclose

In the November 2018 National Science Mathematics Power Quiz and Research Congress, I achieved the notable distinction of being awarded the Bronze Medal in the General Mathematics category. This recognition signifies my proficiency and excellence in the field of General Mathematics. It reflects my commitment to academic achievement and participation in competitive events within the realm of science and mathematics.

Science Innovative Research
Science Innovative Researchclose

In October 2019, I engaged in the Science Innovative Research competition and emerged as the Philippine Qualifier for the 6th World Innovative Technology Challenge to Australia (WIT 2019). Our prototype design, AMAZOBOT, designed to autonomously plant trees and plants, was inspired by the 2019 Amazon fires and the threat they posed to one of Earth's primary oxygen sources. Notably, We secured a Silver Medal in the Innovative Research category during this qualifying stage, underscoring the recognition of my impactful and forward-thinking work.
