Titans - DC Comics Superheroes and Their Adversaries

Titans is an American television series produced for the DC Universe streaming service, later Max, with international distribution via Netflix. The weekly, hour-long series was created by Akiva Goldsman, Geoff Johns, and Greg Berlanti, and began on October 12, 2018.
